## 📥 HOW TO DOWNLOAD

### Method 1: University VPN (Recommended)
1. Connect to your university VPN
2. Click the URLs above
3. Download PDFs directly

### Method 2: ResearchGate (For Paywalled Papers)
1. Go to https://www.researchgate.net
2. Search for paper title
3. Many authors post free preprints

### Method 3: Google Scholar
1. Go to https://scholar.google.com
2. Search: `"Paper Title" author name`
3. Look for [PDF] link on the right

### Method 4: Contact Authors
1. Find author email on paper's webpage
2. Request a copy directly (researchers usually respond)
