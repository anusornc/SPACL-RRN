## Red Flags to Check

Look for these warning signs in the manuscript:

1. **Vague citations**: "Smith et al. proposed a method" without specifics
2. **Overgeneralization**: "Many researchers have shown..." without specific papers
3. **Missing page numbers**: Claims about specific findings without page refs
4. **Incorrect years**: Papers cited for things published later
5. **Wrong venues**: Conference papers cited as journal papers
6. **Fabricated quotes**: Direct quotes that don't exist in the paper
